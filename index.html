About Us

Committed to securing your world with excellence and integrity.

DKB Anchor Security Services Ltd. is a premier security firm based in Accra, Ghana, dedicated to providing uncompromising protection for businesses, residences, and industrial sectors. With extensive experience and a deep understanding of the local security landscape, we deploy proactive strategies and advanced technology to mitigate risks before they escalate.

Our personnel are rigorously vetted and highly trained to handle diverse security challenges. From manned guarding and specialized electronic surveillance to rapid response and comprehensive risk assessments, we operate with a singular mission: to deliver absolute peace of mind and safety to our clients around the clock.

Corporate Security
